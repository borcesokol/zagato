Our Locations

Discover the stunning destinations where our apartments are located.

Flogita

A peaceful seaside village in Halkidiki with long sandy beaches and calm turquoise waters. Five of our apartments are located here, all within walking distance of the sea.

Nea Kalikratia

A vibrant coastal town with a beautiful promenade, traditional tavernas, and a lively summer atmosphere. Great for those who love both beach and nightlife.

Perea

A popular seaside suburb just south of Thessaloniki. Enjoy easy access to Greece's second-largest city while relaxing by the Thermaic Gulf.

Kriopigi

A gem on the Kassandra peninsula known for its crystal-clear waters, pine forests, and scenic beaches. One of Halkidiki's most beloved destinations.

Afitos

One of the most picturesque traditional villages on the Kassandra peninsula, perched on a cliff with breathtaking views of the Toroneos Gulf. Famous for its stone architecture, narrow streets and charming tavernas.
